AR Markers for Space Experience

Printing Instructions

  1. Print these markers on non-glossy paper
  2. Make sure each marker is at least 3x3 inches (7.5x7.5 cm) in size
  3. Ensure the print is clear with good contrast
  4. Cut out each marker leaving a small white border
  5. Place markers on a flat surface with good lighting
